What can I expect from the Question Bank?

The Question Bank contains real interview questions to the following schools:

BCom at UBC Sauder
BCom at UofT Rotman
Bcom at Schulich York

BA Engineering at UofT
BA Engineering at McMaster
BA Engineering at UofT Waterloo

The questions will be grouped by each school, accompanied by an explanation of its underlying theme.

For example:

"Describe an opportunity when you were able to be personally supportive and reassuring to a person who needed a friend." 

This is a question from Sauder's school of business that seeks to uncover the applicants' Leadership qualities.

What can I expect from the Interview Prep Course? 

The Interview Prep Course is a collection of meticulously scripted video lessons designed to get you ahead of the competiton during your video interview.  Depending on the university you are applying to, the course may range from 10-20 lessons, continuosly updated. Question Bank is also included.

Topics covered:
• an outline for the format of the interview
• traits of a successful interviewee
• in-depth breakdown, and how to identify the different types of questions
• expert answers and in-depth explanations to commonly asked questions
• live interview demos
• how to avoid getting stuck with nothing to say

Bonus:
• tips to strengthen the written part of your application

The VIP Mentoring Program is an in-person, one to one,  comprehensive training package designed to hold your hands through the entire application process. The program will cover everything in the Interview Prep Course but tailored specifically to your personal circumstances to maximize efficacy.
